About me

I’m a multimedia designer with a strong passion for modern digital expression, where aesthetics, functionality, and user experience come together. My work is inspired by the worlds of fashion and lifestyle, and I’m driven by creating visual identities and digital experiences that feel both contemporary and meaningful.

I move confidently between design and frontend development, turning ideas into intuitive and visually engaging solutions. For me, good design is not just about how something looks — it’s about how it feels to interact with. I focus on creating clean, thoughtful interfaces that guide the user naturally and leave a lasting impression. With an eye for detail, composition, and brand storytelling, I develop websites, digital concepts, and visual identities that bring clarity and personality to a project. I’m motivated by working with brands and teams that value creativity and want to stand out through a modern, aesthetic approach.
